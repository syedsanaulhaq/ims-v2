Simple diagram showing how branch requests move through storekeeper check, supervisor decision, admin/tender workflow, and final employee allotment.

2. Request Logged

5. Branch Supervisor Reviews

Unavailable Items

Tender Module

Demand is grouped and assigned to tender.

Final Allotment

After procurement, stock is allotted to employee.

DD Admin

Reviews shortage by item group.

AD Admin

Approves or adjusts quantity.

Required Items Queue

Approved shortage becomes demand.

Tender / PO / Delivery

Procurement receives the required stock.

Inventory Updated

Stock deducted from store and added to employee inventory.

Short rule: Available stock is allotted after branch supervisor approval. Unavailable stock becomes required demand, goes through admin and tender/procurement, then is allotted to the employee after stock is received.
